Intro

Welcome to PyPikachu — a lightweight AI-driven platform designed to identify rare and often overlooked printing errors on Pokémon cards. Our toolset uses real listings from marketplaces like eBay, then applies image processing and machine learning to highlight defects that even sellers might miss. Whether you're a collector, reseller, or hobbyist, this platform helps you catch potential hidden gems.By the way, check out my GitHub.

The project currently supports multiple well-known errors (like the iconic Wartortle Evolution Box defect) and is designed to expand over time. Through a simple web interface, users can submit search criteria, monitor listings, and even be notified when cards matching their criteria appear online — all powered by a backend that fuses Flask, Ngrok, and real-time card analysis.

New Task

Use this page to create a task that monitors online marketplaces for specific Pokémon card defects. Simply choose a known error type (like the Wartortle Evolution Box Error), set your price threshold, and select your preferred marketplace.

Once submitted, your task will periodically scan for matching listings in the background. If any card meets your criteria, you'll receive a notification through your selected contact method. It's a powerful way to spot undervalued or mislisted error cards — all without constantly browsing yourself.
